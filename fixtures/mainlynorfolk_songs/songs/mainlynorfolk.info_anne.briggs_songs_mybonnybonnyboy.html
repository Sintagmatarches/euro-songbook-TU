Daisy Chapman of Buchan sang I Once Had a Boy at the Blairgowrie Festival in August 1969. This recording made by Rod Stradling was included in 2000 on her Musical Traditions anthiology Ythanside. Stradling noted:

There are actually 63 examples of this song in Roud, but mostly from England—only five are from Scotland. Three other sound recordings are known; one from Canada, and Bob Blake (Sussex) and Bob Roberts (Suffolk)—DaisyГўВҖВҷs will be, we think, the only available recording.
