Anne Briggs sang The Recruited Collier on the 1963 theme album The Iron Muse: A Panorama of Industrial Folk Music. This recording was also included on her 1990 Fellside anthology Classic Anne Briggs, on her 1999 Topic anthology A Collection., and on the 2014 GFTU / Topic anthology of songs of resistance, democracy and peace, Voice and Vision.
