Blood, Ice and Ashes

[Kim Edgar, Karine Polwart, Kenny Anderson]

Kim Edgar and Karine Polwart were fed up with Scottish murder ballads where women only had a passive role as cause or victim of the murder. They wanted a woman to get the chance to be the murderer and thus wrote Blood, Ice and Ashes.

Karine Polwart sang Blood, Ice and Ashes in 2010 on the Burns Unit album Side Show and in 2011 on her DVD Hereâs Where Tomorrow Starts.
