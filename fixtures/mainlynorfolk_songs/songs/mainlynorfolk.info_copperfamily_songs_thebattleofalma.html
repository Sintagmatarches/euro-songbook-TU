The Battle of Alma

[ Roud 1221 ; Ballad Index DalC216 ; trad.]

The Cruel Wars

The Battle of Alma is a song from the repertoire of the Copper Family. It is printed in The Copper Family Song Book. Bob and John Copper sang it in 1971 on their Leader box set A Song for Every Season.
