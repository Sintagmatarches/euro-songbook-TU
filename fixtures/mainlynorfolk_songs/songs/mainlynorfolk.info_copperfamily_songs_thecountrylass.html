The Country Lass / Brisk and Bonny Lad

[ Roud 606 ; Master title: The Country Lass ; Ballad Index K244 ; Bodleian Roud 606 ; GlosTrad Roud 606 ; Wiltshire 117 ; trad.]

Brisk and Bonny Lad is a song from the repertoire of the Copper Family. Bob and Jim Copper sang it in a BBC recording (BBC 16062) made by Brian George at the Central Club, Peacehaven, on 1 March 1951. This recording was included in 1955 (with the title The Contented Country Lad) on the anthology The Columbia World Library of Folk and Primitive Music - Volume III: England and in 2001 on the Copper Familyâs Topic anthology Come Write Me Down.

John Copper sang Brisk and Bonny Lad in a recording made in November 1957 for the BBC LP 26349.

Bob and John Copper recorded Brisk and Bonny Lad in 1971 for the Copper Familyâs four-album box on the Leader label, A Song for Every Season.

Bob, Jill and John Copper and Jon Dudley sang Brisk and Bonny Lad in 1995 on their CD Coppersongs 2.

Jeff Wesley of Northamptonshire sang Brisk and Bonny Lad, in a recording made by John Howson in possibly 1988, as the title track of his 1988 Veteran Tapes cassette Brisk & Bonny Lad. This track was also included in 1993 on the Veteran anthology CD of traditional folk music, songs and dances from England, Stepping It Out.
