Midsummer Carol

[ Roud 6913 ; VWML SBG/3/1/439 , SBG/3/1/440 ; Folkinfo 505 ; trad.]

Rev. Sabine Baring-Gould collected the Midsummer Carol in October 1890 from William Aggett of Chagford, Devon [VWML SBG/3/1/439] , and printed it in his book Songs of the West. He also collected it on 7 July 1891 from Samuel Gilbert of Mawgan in Pydar, Cornwall [VWML SBG/3/1/440] .

Cyril Tawney sang the Midsummer Carol from Devon in 1973 on his Argo album I Will Give My Love.
