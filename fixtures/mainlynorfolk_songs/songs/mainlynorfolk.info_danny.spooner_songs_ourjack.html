Our Jack

[ Roud 1983 ; Ballad Index Doe169 ; Mudcat 7388 ; trad.]

Martyn Wyndham-Read and Danny Spooner sang Our Jack in 1989 on their album All Around Down Under, and Danny Spooner sang Our Jack in 2008 on his CD Brave Bold Boys. He noted:

This little urban gem was collected by Joy Durst and Ron Edwards in August 1956 from a Mr R. Ure of Gembrook Victoria. It reminds me of some of my old mates in the East End of London.
