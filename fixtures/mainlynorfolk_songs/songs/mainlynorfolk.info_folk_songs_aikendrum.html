Aikendrum (Jacobite song)

[ Roud 2571 ; G/D 8:1694 ; Ballad Index RcAikDr1 ; DT AIKNDRUM ; Mudcat 62938 ; trad.]

The Singing Island

Ewan MacColl and Peggy Seeger sang Aikendrum in 1956 on their Tradition album Classic Scots Ballads. MacColl noted:

This spirited Jacobite song refers to the state of the Whig and Jacobite armies immediately previous to the battle of Sheriffmuir, and the intent of the song was to deprecate (and depreciate) the power and courage of the Whigs.
