Angelina Baker

[ Roud 18341 , 17854 ; Ballad Index BWV4047 ; Bodleian Roud 18341 ; AngelineBaker at Old Songs ; DT ANGELINE ; Mudcat 6169 ; Stephen Collins Foster, 1850]

Angelina Baker is a song written by Stephen Collins Foster for the Christy Minstrels, and published in 1850. It laments the loss of a woman slave, sent away by her owner.

Art Rosenbaum sang Angelina the Baker in a concert at Wilder Main, Oberlin College, on 11 October 1975. This recording was included in 1976 on the anthology of Oberlin College Folk Music Club live recordings, The Audience Pleased.

Sam Kelly & The Lost Boys sang Angelina the Baker in 2017 on their Navigator CD Pretty Peggy.

The Norfolk Broads sang Angelina Baker on their 2017 CD In the Valley of the Flowers.
