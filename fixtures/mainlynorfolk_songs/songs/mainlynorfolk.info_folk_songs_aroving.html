Stanley Slade with a male chorus sang A-Roving in the BBC recording 6018 made in Bristol on 2 July 1943. This track was included in 1955 on the Columbia anthology The World Library of Folk and Primitive Music: England.

Paul Clayton sang The Maid of Amsterdam in 1956 on his Tradition album Whaling and Sailing Songs From the Days of Moby Dick. He noted:
