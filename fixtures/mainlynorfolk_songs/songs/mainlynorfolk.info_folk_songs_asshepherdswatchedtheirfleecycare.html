As Shepherds Watched Their Fleecy Care

[ Roud 1518 ; Ballad Index PECS142 ; Joseph Key]

Roy Palmer: Everymanâs Book of English Country Songs

As Shepherds Watched Their Fleecy Care is a song written by Joseph Key and first published in 1785 in Five Anthems, Four Collects, Twenty Psalm Tunes. Roy Palmer had a nearly identical version of The Fleecy Care, but without the chorus, in his 1979 Everymanâs Book of English Country Songs.
