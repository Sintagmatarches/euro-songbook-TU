Died for Love

[ Roud 18828 ; Master title: Died for Love ; Ballad Index Gard028 ; Go Dig My Grave / Died for Love / The Butcher Boy at Fire Draw Near ; DT DIEDLOV3 ; Mudcat 161176 ; trad.]

Steve Gardham: A Yorkshire Songster Fred Hamer: Green Groves Mary and Nigel Hudleston: Songs of the Ridings

This is the Died for Love variant in which the father finds his deserted and pregnant daughter having hanged herself. For the most common version with this title see The Brisk Young Sailor / Died for Love (Roud 60).

This recording was included in 2010 on the Veteran anthology of Harry Green and other traditional singers from Essex with songs, tunes and stepdances from the Sam Steele collection, The Fox & the Hare.
