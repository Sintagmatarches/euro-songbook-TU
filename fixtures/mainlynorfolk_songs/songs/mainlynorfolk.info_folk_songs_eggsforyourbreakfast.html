Eggs for Your Breakfast / The Pretty Little Duck / Country Life

[ Roud 1752 ; Master title: Eggs for Your Breakfast ; G/D 8:1667 ; Ballad Index Grd81667 ; Mudcat 120708 ; trad. / Harry Linn]

Walter Pardon sang A Country Life at home in a recording made by Mike Yates on 2 August 1978. It was published in 1982 as the title track of his Topic LP A Country Life and in 2000 on his Musical Traditions anthology Put a Bit of Powder on It, Father.
