I Wish I Was a Mole in the Ground

[ Roud 4957 ; Ballad Index BAF900 ; DT WISHMOLE ; Mudcat 18014 ; trad.]

Bob Neuwirth with Eliza Carthy sang I Wish I Was a Mole in the Ground, recorded at Meltdown Festival, Royal Festival Hall, London, on 2 July 1999, wich was included in 2006 on the anthology of American folk music revisited album, The Harry Smith Project.

Matt Gordon and Leonard Podolak with Bill Shanley sang Mole in the Ground in 2012 on their Rootbeat album Three Thins Dimes.

Alden Patterson and Dashwood sang Mole in the Ground in 2016 on their first trio album Call Me Home.
