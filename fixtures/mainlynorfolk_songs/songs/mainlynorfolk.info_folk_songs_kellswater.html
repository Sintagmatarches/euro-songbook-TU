Irish duo Lumiere sang Kellswater in 2009 on their eponymous Sony CD Lumiere. They noted:

The woman in this song is very much in love and she fears that her man is going to be taken across the sea from her. It is the honesty of the love that she feels for him that attracted us to this song in the first place.The pace flows along like a river and the song ends on a positive note 

Kellswater is in Co. Antrim in the north of Ireland.
