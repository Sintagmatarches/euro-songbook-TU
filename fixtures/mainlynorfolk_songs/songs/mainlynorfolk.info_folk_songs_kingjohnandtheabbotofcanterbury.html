King John and the Abbot of Canterbury

[ Roud 302 ; Child 45 ; G/D 2:281 ; Ballad Index C045 ; Bodleian Roud 302 ; Mudcat 159270 ; trad.]

John Jacob Niles: The Ballad Book of John Jacob Niles

Chris Foster sang King John and the Abbot of Canterbury in 1979 on his Topic album All Things in Common. He noted:

A song about a clash between church and state, including a shepherd with a misplaced sense of loyalty, three good riddles and a dubious happy ending.
