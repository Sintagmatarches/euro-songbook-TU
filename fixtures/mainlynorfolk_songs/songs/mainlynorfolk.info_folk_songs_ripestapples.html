Collected by Lucy Broadwood in Attleborough, Norfolk. A song obviously collected by a woman, from the singing of a woman. A spirited song of rejection. The protagonist snubs the advances of a man who assumes he can seduce her with his riches. Most prolific song collectors of the past were male, collecting songs in male dominated spaces. Lucy Broadwood was one of few collectors approaching women in their home environments.
