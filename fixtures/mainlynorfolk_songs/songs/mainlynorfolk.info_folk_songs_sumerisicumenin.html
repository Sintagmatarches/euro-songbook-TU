Sumer Is I-cumen In

[ RoudBS B258230 ; Ballad Index FSWB260B ; DT ICUMSUM ; Mudcat 13004 ; trad.]

Sumer Is I-cumen In is a round celebrating the beginning of summer and the appearance of various symbols of fertility. It is printed in the British Museum manuscript Harley 978, generally dated c.1225-1250.
