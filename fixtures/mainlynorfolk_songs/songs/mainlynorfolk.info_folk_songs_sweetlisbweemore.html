The Songs of Elizabeth Cronin

Patrick Street sang Sweet Lisbweemore, a song about a failed seduction, in 1978 on their Green Linnet album Cornerboys. Andy Irvine noted:

This extremely witty song becomes more so when one realises it was written by “that man they call D.D.” who the girl is frightened will write a compromising song about her and the young man who is showing her the way. A turbary is—or was—the right of digging turf on common or private property.
