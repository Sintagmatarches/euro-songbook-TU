Tak a Dram (Afor Ye Go)

[ Roud - ; Ian Sinclair]

Mirk sang Tak a Dram (Afore Ye Go), written by band member Ian Sinclair from Caithness, as the title track of their 1982 Springthyme album Tak a Dram Afore Ye Go. They noted:

A song that celebrates the Scots custom of offering a visitor the hospitality of the house before he leaves. Words and music are by Ian Sinclair. From most parts of Scotland it is a long road north to Caithness. So, after a night of good music, when we have to face a long road home, we like to finish the night with this song.
