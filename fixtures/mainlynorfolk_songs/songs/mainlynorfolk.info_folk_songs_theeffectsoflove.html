The song also exists as broadsides, under the title The Effects of Love, and Steve Gardham gives a detailed history on The Yorkshire Garland Group website.

I have been drawn to this song for some time, but it did not feel quite right. Therefore, I took some of Mr Cooperâs version and added in parts from the broadsides, I gave it a new tune and this is the result. Betsy may or may not have been a real person but society can be a cruel place for anyone who has found themselves in a similar position. This one is for all the women who felt like they had no other choice.

The waves you can hear are the sounds of the River Humber, recorded on 21 February 2022 during Storm Franklin.
