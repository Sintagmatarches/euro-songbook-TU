Derek and Dorothy Elliott sang Tally Ho the Hounds in 1972 on their eponymous Leader album Derek & Dorothy Elliott.

Leslie Garside of the Holme Valley Beagles sang Doctor Mack at the Village Hall, Upperthong, Yorkshire, on 24 March 1973. This track was included in 1975 on the Leader album A Fine Hunting Day.

Will Noble sang Doctor Mack on his 2017 album It芒聙聶s Gritstone for Me. Brian Peters and John Howson noted:

Another song from the Holme Valley Beagles. The Reg Hall collection in the British Library online sound archive includes a 1973 recording of Leslie Garside singing it at one of their meets, but Will heard it sung regularly at the sessions by Stuart Kaye. “I always liked the humour in it.”

Dave Lowry sang Parson Hogg on his 2024 WildGoose album Songs of a Devon Man. Bill Crawford noted:

Sabine Baring-Gould learnt this from his uncle, Thomas Franklin of Franklin House in Exeter—now a physiotherapy centre, and part of the NHS.
