The Huntsman

[trad.]

Former Witch of Elswick, Fay Hield sang the Forest School Camp song The Huntsman in 2010 on her first solo CD, Looking Glass. Fay and Jon Boden also sang The Huntsman as the 2 October 2010 entry of his project A Folk Song a Day.
