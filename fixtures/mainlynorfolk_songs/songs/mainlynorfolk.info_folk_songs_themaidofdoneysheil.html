The Maid of Doneysheil

[ Roud 6894 ; Henry H530 ; Ballad Index HHH530 ; Paddy McGuckian]

Malinky sang The Maid of Doneysheil in 2019 on their 20th anniversary album Handsel. They noted:

This song, written by Paddy McGuckian, was collected in Ballymacaldrick, Antrim, and published as part of Sam Henryâs Songs of the People in January 1934. The hero of the song was Tom McLaughlin from Kilcreen; according to the source he did not win the heroine, and neither did he reach Nova Scotia. We have altered the published version of the song a little, with a verse from another song incorporated for narrative purposes. Doneysheil is on the east bank of the Bann, located between Rasharkin and Kilrea.
