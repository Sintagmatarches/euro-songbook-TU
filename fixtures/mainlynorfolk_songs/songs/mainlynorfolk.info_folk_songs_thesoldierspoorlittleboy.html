Bryony Griffith and Alice Jones sang Early Pearly on their 2022 Christmas / homeless charities single Early Pearly and on their 2023 album of winter songs and tunes from Yorkshire, Wesselbobs. They noted:

Early Pearly is a traditional Yorkshire song that was still being sung by children in the 1940s in the streets of Ripon at Christmas time. The jaunty tune shrouds the sentimentality typical of Victorian tearjerkers, and the children would go from door to door in the hope of tugging on the heartstrings of the occupants to gain financial reward.
