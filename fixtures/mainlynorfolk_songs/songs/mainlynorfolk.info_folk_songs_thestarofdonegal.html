The Star of Donegal

Colm O Lochlainn printed The Star of Donegal as sung by P. Walsh of Clogher Valley, Co. Tyrone, in his 1939 book Irish Street Ballads.

John McGettigan sang The Star of Donegal in a 1936 recording made in Philadelphia. It was included in 1979 on the Topic album of classic recordings of Irish traditional music in America, John McGettigan and His Irish Minstrels.
