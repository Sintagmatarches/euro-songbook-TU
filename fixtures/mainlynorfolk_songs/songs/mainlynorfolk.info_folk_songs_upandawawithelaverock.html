Quinie sang Up and Awa and Awa Wi the Laverock on her 2018 cassette Buckie Prins. She noted:

This song describes the pleasures of line-fishing, and gives locations where good fish are to be found. The singer rises with the laverock [lark] in the morning and takes to the hills.
