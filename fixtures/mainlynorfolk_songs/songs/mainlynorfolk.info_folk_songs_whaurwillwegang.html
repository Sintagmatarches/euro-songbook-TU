Whaur Will We Gang

[ Roud - ; DT WHARGANG ; Mudcat 25159 ; Andy Mitchell]

Andy Mitchell sang his song Whaur Will Whe Gang on the digital Bandcamp album The Land We Call Home that accompanied his book of the same name.

Cilla Fisher sang song Whaur Will We Gang in 1983 on her Kettle album Songs of the Fishing.

Ross Kennedy sang Whaur Will We Gang in 2007 on his Greentrax album Scottish Voice and Acoustic Guitar. He noted:

I originally heard this excellent Andy Mitchell song sung unaccompanied, a beautiful and poignant song, lamenting the loss of the fishing industry.
