Willow Glen

[ Roud V1634 ; VWML FK/18/1/2 , FK/16/89/3 ; Bodleian Roud V1634 ; trad.]

Fay Hield found Willow Glen in the Lucy Broadwood Broadside Collection and sang it on her 2016 album Old Adam. Willow Glen was also released on a digital download single in October 2015. This video shows her with the Full English Band at Cecil Sharp House on 31 October 2013:
