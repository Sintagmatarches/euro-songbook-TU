Yorkshire Couple

[ Roud - ; DT YORKCOUP ; Mudcat 23140 ; Jim Mackie]

Kate Rusby sang the cheeky Yorkshire Couple (to the tune of the Female Drummer) unaccompanied in the Andy Kershaw Show on BBC Radio 1, broadcast on 1 December 1997. She also sang it live at Leeds City Varieties Music Hall in September 2002. The recording of this concert was released in 2004 on her DVD Live From Leeds. She returned to The Yorkshire Couple on her 2025 album When They All Looked Up on which she noted:
