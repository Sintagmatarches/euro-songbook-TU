Young Simon John

[ Roud - ; Master title: Young Simon John ; TYG 77 ; Mudcat 135537 ; Tom Daniel]

Bill Price recorded Young Simon John in 1972 for his Folk Heritage album The Fine Old Yorkshire Gentleman. His sleeve notes commented:

He always insisted that the audience join him in the actions of donning the “neetcap, neetshirt and long-stockings”, and that “Mm-mm”, was added at the end of every verse, as it was the best part of the song.
