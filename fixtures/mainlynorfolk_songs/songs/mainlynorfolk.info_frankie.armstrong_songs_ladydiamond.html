Jean Redpath sang Lady Dysie in 1975 on her eponymous album Jean Redpath. She noted:

I heard the story sung by Winnie Campbell and was intrigued by it. Through the judicious use of a tape-recorder (I find it impossible to learn a song from paper—it never seems to ‘fit’) I managed to get this combination of BronsonГўВҖВҷs first printed melody and a collated text to feel almost as comfortable as the well-worn ballads I like to sing.
