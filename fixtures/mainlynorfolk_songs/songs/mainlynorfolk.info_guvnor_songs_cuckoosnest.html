The CuckooГўВҖВҷs Nest is a song and a Morris Dance tune.

John Strachan sang a fragment of The CuckooГўВҖВҷs Nest to Alan Lomax and Hamish Henderson in Fyvie, Aberdeenshire, Scotland, on 16 July 1951. It was included in 2002 on his Rouder album in the Alan Lomax Collection, Songs From Aberdeenshire.
