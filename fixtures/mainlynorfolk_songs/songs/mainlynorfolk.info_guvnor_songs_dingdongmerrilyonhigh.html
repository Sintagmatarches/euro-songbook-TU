Ding Dong! Merrily on High (Branle de lâOfficiel)

[words George Ratcliffe Woodward (1848-1934), music Thoinot Arbeau (1520-95)]

The Albion Band sang The Official Branle (Ding Dong Merrily on High) in 1985 on their album A Christmas Present From the Albion Band. A live recording from their Christmas Tour 1986 was published in 2010 on their Talking Elephant CD Another Christmas Present From the Albion Band.

Maddy Prior and the Carnival Band sang Ding Dong! Merrily on High (Branle de lâOfficiel) to a traditional French tune on their CDs A Tapestry of Carols (1987), Carols at Christmas, (1998), and An Evening of Carols and Capers (2005).

BACCApella (the singers of Bacca Pipes Folk Club; amongst them at the time were Maggie Boyle, Lynda Hardcastle, Fay Hield, Mike and Helen Hockenhull, and Tim Moon) sang Ding Dong! Merrily on High in 1999 on their privately released CD The Haworth Set.

Coope Boyes & Simpson, Fi Fraser, Jo Freya, and Georgina Boyes sang Ding Dong! Merrily on High in 2006 on their No Masters CD Voices at the Door.

GreenMatthews sang The Official Branle (Ding! Dong Merrily on High) on their 2015 CD A Brief History of Christmas.

A Winter Union sang Ding Dong! Merrily on High on their eponymous 2016 CD A Winter Union. A live recording from Otford Memorial Hall, Kent, on 21 December 2018 was released a year later on their download album Live in Concert.
