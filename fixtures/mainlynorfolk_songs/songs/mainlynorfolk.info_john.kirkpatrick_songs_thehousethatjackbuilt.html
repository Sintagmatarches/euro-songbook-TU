The House That Jack Built

[ Roud 12921 , 20584 ; Ballad Index RcTHTJBu ; trad.]

Valentine Dyall and children of Brittania Row sang The House That Jack Built in 1982 on the video 70 Golden Nursery Rhymes.

Charlie Wills of Pothills, Ryall, Dorset sang The House That Jack Built in his home in January 1971 to Bill Leader and Robin Teague. This recording was included in 1998 on the Topic anthology To Catch a Fine Buck Was My Delight (The Voice of the People Volume 18).
