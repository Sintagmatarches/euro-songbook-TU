Andy M. Stewart sang his own song Where Are You Tonight, I Wonder? in 1987 on his and Manus Lunnyâs Green Linnet album Dublin Lady.

June Tabor sang Where Are You Tonight, I Wonder? in 1988 on her Topic album Aqaba.

Niamh Parsons sang Where Are You Tonight, I Wonder? in 1992 on her Greentrax album Loosely Connected.

Michelle Burke sang Where Are You Tonight, I Wonder? on her 2009 album Pulling Threads.

Alistair Ogilvy sang Where Are You Tonight, I Wonder? in 2012 on his Greentrax album Leaves Sae Green. He noted:

A heartrending song of unrequited love, written by the prolific Andy M. Stewart.

Hannah Rarity sang Where Are You Tonight, I Wonder? on her 2016 EP Beginnings and on her 2018 CD Neath the Gloaming Star.
