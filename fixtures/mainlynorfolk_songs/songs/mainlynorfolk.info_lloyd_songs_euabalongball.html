Euabalong Ball

[ Roud 38579 ; AFS 35 ; Ballad Index RcEBALL ; trad.]

A.L. Lloyd sang Euabalong Ball in 1956 on his Riverside album Australian Bush Songs and in 1957 on his Australian EP with Ewan MacColl, Convicts and Currency Lads.
