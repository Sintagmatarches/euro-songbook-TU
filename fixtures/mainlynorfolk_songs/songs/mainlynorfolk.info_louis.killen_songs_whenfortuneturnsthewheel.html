Jackie Oates learned When Fortune Turns the Wheel from Louis Killen and recorded it in 2011 for her album Saturnine. She noted:

Taught to me by Lou Killen, this song has long been one of the life-affirming songs that I love to sing in company. The wheel of fortune is a concept from ancient and medieval philosophy, referring to the capricious nature of Fate. The wheel belongs to the goddess Fortuna who spins it at random, changing the positions of those on the wheel with some suffering great misfortune while others gain windfalls. Fortune appears on all paintings as a woman, sometimes blindfolded, ‘puppeteering” a wheel.
