Duncan and Brady

[ Roud 4177 ; Laws I9 ; Ballad Index LI09 ; Huddie Ledbetter]

Martin Carthy, Rory McEwen and Lisa Turner sang two Lead Belly songs, this one, i.e. Duncan and Brady, and My Girl, on the 1963 Hullabaloo ABC Television programme. Both tracks were released in 2001 on The Carthy Chronicles.

Martin Simpson sang Duncan and Brady in 2007 on his Topic album Prodigal Son.

Rattle on the Stovepipe sang Duncan and Brady in 2014 on their WildGoose CD Old Virginia. They noted:

Officer James Brady was fatally shot on 6 October 1890 when police entered Charles Starkesâs Saloon in downtown St Louis, Missouri, to break up a fight. Tried for murder, bartender Harry Duncan protested his innocence but, despite an appeal to the US Supreme Court, was hanged in 1894. There were rumours of a deathbed confession by Charles Starkes himself. The song was first recorded by Wilmer Watts and his Lonely Eagles in 1929.
