Early One Morning

[ Roud 12682 ; Ballad Index FSWB185 ; Bodleian Roud 12682 ; DT EARLY1AM , EARLY1A3 ; Mudcat 33631 ; trad.]

Martin Carthy and Maggie Goodall sang Early One Morning, a song of courting and abandonment, in 1984 on the album The Old Songs.

Isla St Clair sang Early One Morning, on her 2002 album My Generation.
