Master Kilby

[ Roud 1434 ; Ballad Index KaCr060 ; VWML CJS2/10/2042 ; Mudcat 58570 ; trad.]

Cecil Sharp collected the love song Master Kilby from Harry Richards of Curry Rivel in Somerset both on 29 July 1904 and on 6 January 1909.

Nic Jones found Master Kilby in the Journals of the English Folk Song Society and recorded it for his 1978 Transatlantic / The Leader Tradition album From the Devil to a Stranger. Two live performances were released on his CDs Unearthed (2001) and Game Set Match (2006).
