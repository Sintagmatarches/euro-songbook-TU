The Leaves in the Woodland

[Peter Bellamy]

June Tabor sang the role of the Mother in Peter Bellamyâs ballad opera The Transports. In the 1992 Whitby Folk Festival performance of The Transports, Norma Waterson took over the role of the Mother.

Grace Notes sang The Leaves in the Woodland on the 2004 re-recording of The Transports.

Peter and Barbara Snape sang The Leaves in the Woodland on their 2011 CD Revel & Rally. Barbara Snape noted:

From The Transports—A Ballad Opera written by Peter Bellamy. The song was sung by the mother (June Tabor) in the original recording in 1977 and it is a most beautiful and haunting song.
