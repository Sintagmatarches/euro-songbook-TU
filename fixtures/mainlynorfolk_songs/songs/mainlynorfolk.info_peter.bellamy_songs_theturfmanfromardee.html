The Turfman From Ardee

[ Roud 5187 ; Ballad Index RcTurArd ; Mudcat 3442 ; trad.]

Colm O Lochlainn: More Irish Street Ballads

John McGettigan sang The Turfman From Ardee in a 1936 recording made in Philadelphia. It was included in 1979 on the Topic album of classic recordings of Irish traditional music in America, John McGettigan and His Irish Minstrels.

Margaret Barry sang The Turfman From Ardee in a recording made by Ewan MacColl in Croydon on 10 March 1955, on her 1965 Topic album Her Mantle So Green. A recording from October 1965 in New York was included in 1975 on the Folkways album Margaret Barry and Michael Gorman.

Peter Bellamy learned The Turfman From Ardee from the singing of Margaret Barry. He recorded it in 1979 for his Topic LP Both Sides Then, accompanying himself on guitar.
