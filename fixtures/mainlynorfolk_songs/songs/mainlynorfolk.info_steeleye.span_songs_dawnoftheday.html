Dawn of the Day

[Steeleye Span]

Even though Steeleye Span never released Dawn of the Day on any studio album, it was in their live set in around 1991. A concert recording from that time was included on their CD Tonightâs the Night… Live. The place of the recording was not mentioned in the sleeve notes and neither was the song credited. It sounds as it might have been written by Rick Kemp.

Jon Boden sang The Dawn of the Day as the 12 October 2010 entry of his project A Folk Song a Day.
