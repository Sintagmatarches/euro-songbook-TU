In December 1972, a different edit of Gaudete, without the Cathedral-like acoustics, was issued as a Steeleye Span single with the B-side The Holly and the Ivy. The song has appeared on many compilations over the years but with the exception of the Original Masters 2 CD set, the single version seems to have become lost in favour of the album version. An original tape copy was found in the Festival Records tape vaults and has been fully sound restored for reissue on the 1999 CD A Rare Collection 1972-1996.
