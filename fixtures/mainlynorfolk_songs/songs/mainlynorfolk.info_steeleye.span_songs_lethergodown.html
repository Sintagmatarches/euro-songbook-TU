Let Her Go Down

[ Roud - ; Mudcat 14798 ; Peter Knight]

Steeleye Span sang Let Her Go Down in 1980 on their reunion album Sails of Silver. This recording was also released as B-side of their single Gone to America. A live recording from the Royal Opera Theatre in Adelaide, Australia in 1982 was released on the rare Australian-only LP On Tour.
