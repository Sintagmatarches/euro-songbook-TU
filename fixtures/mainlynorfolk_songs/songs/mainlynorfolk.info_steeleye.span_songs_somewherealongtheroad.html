A new recording of this song was included in 2015 on the album Catch Up—The Essential Steeleye Span.

Maddy Prior recorded Somewhere Along the Road for a second time in 1993 for her solo album Year.

And finally Rick Kemp sang Somewhere Along the Road himself on his 1996 solo album Escape. He was accompanied by Ian Kellet, who was programming and playing keyboards, and by John Kirkpatrick on accordion. He and his daughter Theresa Kemp also sang it in 2018 on his Fellside album Perfect Blue.

Cathie Ryan (of Cherish the Ladies) sang Somewhere Along the Road in 2001 as the title track of her CD Somewhere Along the Road with Kate Rusby singing harmony vocals.

Sangsters sang Somewhere Along the Road in 2000 on their Greentrax album Sharp and Sweet.

Hen Party sang Somewhere Along the Road in 2002 on their WildGoose album The Heart Gallery. Sarah Margan also sang it to her own piano accompaniment in a home recording that was included in 2014 on her posthumous retrospective Only Remembered.

Marsden Blant & Squire recorded Somewhere Along the Road in 2003 for their album Trio.
