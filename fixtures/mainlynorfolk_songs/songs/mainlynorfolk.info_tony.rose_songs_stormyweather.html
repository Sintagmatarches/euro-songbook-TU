Tony Rose sang Stormy Weather live in Cheltenham in 1967. This recording was included in 2008 on his posthumous CD Exe.

Harold Smy from Ipswich, Suffolk. sang Stormy Weather Boys in 1985 to John Howson. This recording was included in 1999 on the Veteran Tapes cassette Songs Sung in Suffolk Vol 5 and in 2001 on the Veteran anthology of traditional folk music from Coastal England, When the Wind Blows.
