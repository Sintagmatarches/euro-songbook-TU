The Watersons sang the Swinton May Song in 1975 on their album For Pence and Spicy Ale. A.L. Lloyd noted:

On holidays such as Christmas, May Day, etc., people would dress in what finery they had, hence the airy picture of the master and mistress of the house with their gold chains and brooches, very flattering for poor cottagers.
