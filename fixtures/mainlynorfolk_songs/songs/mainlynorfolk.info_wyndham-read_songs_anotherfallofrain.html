Another Fall of Rain

[ Roud 22614 ; AFS 3 ; Ballad Index MA154 ; DT FALLRAIN ; John Neilson]

Martyn Wyndham-Read sang Another Fall of Rain on his 2013 CD Starlit Skies. He noted:

A song that goes back many years for me. Just recently I played it with a different rhythm and it took on a new life. The beauty of these old songs is that they will withstand any interpretation and still come back to the same shape and form. The song may be based on the poem by the Australian poet John Shaw Neilson to a tune of his time: The Little Low Log Cabin in the Lane.

Danny Spooner sang Another Fall of Rain in 2017 on his final CD, Home. He noted:

Also known as Waiting for the Rain, John Meredith collected a version from wharfie Leo Dixon, who had been a bush worker and shearer and was born at Eugowra. Meredith stated that the words were written by John Neilson of Penola, a bush worker, farmer, and balladist, and the father of John Shaw Neilson.

The last verse in this version was sent me by email and comes from Dave de Hugard"s record Freedom on the Wallaby.
