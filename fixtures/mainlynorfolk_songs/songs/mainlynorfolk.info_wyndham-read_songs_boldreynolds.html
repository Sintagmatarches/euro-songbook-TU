Bold Reynolds

[ Roud - ; Mudcat 5262 ; Dave Toye]

Martyn Wyndham-Read sang Dave Toyeâs song Bold Reynolds in 1992 on his Fellside album Mussels on a Tree. He noted:

A song about foxhunting from the point of view of the fox. I hope any foxes listening will excuse shortcomings in the translation from the fox tongue into English. The human author responsible is Dave Toye, a Horsham man living in Sussex (many miles from Aberystwyth) and a friend of more years than I care to admit to.

Damien Barber sang Bold Reynolds on his 1999 Promotional CD and on his 2000 album The Furrowed Field. He noted:

When I was growing up in Norfolk I would meet up with my friends at the weekends and chase ‘dumb’ animals around the countryside. Our quarry was a very aggressive beast and we rarely brought one down, mainly due to the fact that they rode on horseback, had whips and thought it was their right to oppress anything they didnât like. I got this song from Martin Wyndham-Read, one of my favourite singers.
