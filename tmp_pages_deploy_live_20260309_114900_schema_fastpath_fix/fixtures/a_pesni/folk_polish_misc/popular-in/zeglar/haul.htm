Szanta “krótko szarpana" - Short-Haul-Shanty

When I was а little lad,
So my mother told me,
'Way, haul away, we'll haul away, Joe!

That if I didn't kiss the gals
Му lips would all grow mouldy.
'Way, haul...

The cook is in the galley
Making chow so handy,

The capitan's in his cabin
Pulling on his brandy.

King Louis was the king of France
Before the revolution,

King Louis got his head cut off
And spoiled his constitution.

'Way, haul away,
We'll haul away the bowline.

'Way, haul away,
The packet is а-rolling.

'Way, haul away,
We'll haul for better weather,

'Way, haul away,
Wе'll hang and haul together.
